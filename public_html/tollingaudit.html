Tolling Video Player
.NET video player for auditing tolling systems

This application is a C# .NET video player designed to help customers audit automated highway tolling systems. It synchronizes the CCTV camera footage of the highway with the transaction data generated by the toll point. This allows customers to easily compare the video to the transactions and quickly skip between video time stamps as each car passes through the toll point.

Features:

  • Synchronizes CCTV camera footage with tolling system transaction data
  • Allows for instant skipping between video time stamps to exactly when cars pass through
  • Displays tolling transaction data in text overlay over video to easily compare against video
  • Automatically determines date and time of both video and transaction data files
  • Allows customers to audit automated highway tolling systems quickly, accurately, and easily
